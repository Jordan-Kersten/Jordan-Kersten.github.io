Jordan is a classical and contemporary voice teacher in the SF Bay Area.  She draws on her extensive training and experience as a singer, actor, performer and movement professional to help her students unleash their vocal potential.  She believes firmly that singing should be enjoyable and therapeutic, and that a combination of focus and play are key to unlocking the voice.

During our first lesson, we'll discuss your vocal  background, as well as your goals and hopes for our work together.  Then, we'll warm you up and explore your voice. All levels and all voice types are welcome, including absolute beginners, advanced students, and crossover singers.


Jordan has studied with voice teachers Mary Dunkel, Juliana Snapper, Debra Lambert and Mindy Lym during her 20+ years of training.  She has trained with Susan Jane Harrison in Linklater technique and voice building for actors.  Jordan holds a BA from UC San Diego (where she studied with Ms. Snapper), and she completed the Vocal Pedagogy Professional Workshop at Boston Conservatory at Berklee in 2019.
